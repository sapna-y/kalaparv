About The Event

The Biggest Cultural Celebration

Kalaparv 2026 is A Festival of Creative Excellence organized by Dream Dance Academy that brings together artists, dancers, singers, and creative minds from across the region. This is your stage to shine – whether you're a solo performer, part of a group, or a budding artist.

The festival provides exposure at District, State, National & International platforms, giving every participant a chance to take their talent to the world. With expert judges, professional stage setup, and enthusiastic audiences, Kalaparv is where dreams take center stage.
